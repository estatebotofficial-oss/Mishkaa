Refunds & Cancellations

Cancellation

You can stop your subscription at any time by simply not renewing it. There is no lock-in period. Since access is granted for a fixed period (30 days) per payment, there is nothing to actively "cancel" — your access simply ends at the end of the period you already paid for.

Refunds

Because access to the private group is granted immediately upon successful payment, all payments are final and non-refundable, except in the following cases:

1. A duplicate payment was made in error for the same billing period.
2. A technical issue on our end prevented you from receiving access after payment.

If either applies to you, contact us within 7 days of the payment with your payment reference, and we will review and process a refund where applicable within 5-7 business days.

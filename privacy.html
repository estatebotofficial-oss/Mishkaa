What we collect

When you subscribe, we collect your email address and phone number to process payment and manage your subscription. Payment details themselves are handled directly by Cashfree — we never store your card or bank information.

Your contact details are used solely to manage your subscription, confirm payments, and provide access to the private group. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

To join the private group, Telegram may have access to standard account information (such as your Telegram user ID) as governed by Telegram's own privacy policy.

Data retention

We retain subscription records for as long as needed to manage billing and access, and to comply with applicable law.

Contact
